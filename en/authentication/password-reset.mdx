# Password Reset

If you have forgotten your password, you can follow the password reset steps to regain access to the system.

## Password Reset Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Reset Page">
    Click the "Forgot your password?" link on the [Login page](/en/authentication/login) or go directly to `https://app.bultech.co/forgot-password`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Email Address">
    Enter the email address you are registered with and click the "Send Reset Link" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Open the email containing the password reset link sent to your inbox. (Don't forget to check your spam/junk folder).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your New Password">
    On the page that opens when you click the link in the email, set your new password and complete the process by clicking the "Change Password" button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  After successfully changing your password, you will be redirected to the [login page](/en/authentication/login) where you can log in with your new password.
</Info>
