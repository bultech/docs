# Welcome to the Bul Tech User Guide

This guide is designed to help you use the Bul Tech platform effectively. We will explore all the features, modules, and workflows of the platform step by step.

## Getting Started

If you are new to the platform, we recommend following the steps below:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Platform Rules">
    Review the [Platform Rules](/en/platform-rules/limits-and-restrictions) page to learn about the general operation and limits of the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Follow the [Sign Up](/en/authentication/signup) steps to create your account and take the first step onto the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Company">
    Start using the platform by creating your first company with the [Create Company](/en/company-operations/company-creation) guide.
  </Step>
</Steps>
