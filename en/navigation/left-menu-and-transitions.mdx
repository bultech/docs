# Left Menu and Transitions

The left menu is your main navigation tool within the platform. It allows you to switch between companies and projects and access modules.

## Menu Structure

The menu exists in two main states:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Company-Level Navigation">
    Before entering a project, it provides access to company-wide information.
    - **Company Selector:** At the top, there is a selector showing the name and logo of your current company. By clicking it, you can switch to other companies you are a member of or create a new one.
    - **Company Overview (<img src="/icons/view-dashboard-outline.svg" alt="view-dashboard-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />):** Takes you to the [Company Overview Dashboard](/en/company-operations/company-overview).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Project-Level Navigation">
    When you enter a project, the menu changes to show modules specific to that project.
    - **Project Selector:** Below the company selector, an area appears showing the name of the current project. By clicking it, you can quickly switch to other projects within that company.
    - **Project Modules:**
        - **Analytics (<img src="/icons/chart-box-outline.svg" alt="chart-box-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />):** Project analytics dashboard.
        - **Cost Management (<img src="/icons/database-search-outline.svg" alt="database-search-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />):** Includes sub-modules like Budget, Change Orders, and Spending.
        - **Commitments (<img src="/icons/file-sign.svg" alt="file-sign" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />):** Contract log.
        - **Meeting Minutes (<img src="/icons/calendar-outline.svg" alt="calendar-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />):** Area where you manage your meetings.
        - **AI Chat (<img src="/icons/chat-outline.svg" alt="chat-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />):** DinoBot AI assistant.
        - **Daily Log (<img src="/icons/calendar-clock-outline.svg" alt="calendar-clock-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />):** Daily site reports.
        - **Work Management (<img src="/icons/calendar-task-outline.svg" alt="calendar-task-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />):** Task and epic tracking.
        - **Project Settings (<img src="/icons/office-building-cog-outline.svg" alt="office-building-cog-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />):** Project-specific settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## User Card and Settings

At the bottom of the left menu, there is a user card with your profile picture, name, and email. By clicking or hovering over this card, you can access the following from the menu that opens:

- **Profile:** Your [Edit Profile](/en/account-management/edit-profile) page.
- **Settings:** The [General Settings Panel](/en/general-settings/settings-panel).
- **Support:** The [Support Center](/en/general-features/support-center).
- **Log Out:** Allows you to securely log out of your account.
