# Company Settings and Information Update

You can update your company's information at any time.

## Steps to Update Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Settings Page">
    1. Click the **Company** (<img src="/icons/view-dashboard-outline.svg" alt="view-dashboard-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />) icon in the left menu.
    2. Click the **Settings** (<img src="/icons/cog-outline.svg" alt="cog-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />) tab in the top menu.
    
    Direct URL: `https://app.bultech.co/company/:companyId/detail/settings`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make Changes">
    On the settings page, you can update fields such as company name, phone number, and address.
    
    <Note>The company email address cannot be changed for security reasons.</Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save your changes by clicking the blue "Update Information" button at the bottom of the form.
  </Step>
</Steps>
