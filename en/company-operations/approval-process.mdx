# Application and Approval Process

When you create a new company on the Bul Tech platform, it must go through an approval process before you can use all of its features. This process is implemented to ensure the security and quality of the platform.

## How the Approval Process Works

After creating a new company or logging into a company that has not yet been approved, you will be automatically redirected to this page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Application Received">
    When you create your company, your application is automatically received. You will see a progress indicator on the screen confirming that your application has been received.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Will Be Made">
    The Bul Tech team will contact you within 1-3 business days using the contact information you provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approval and Activation">
    After the necessary checks are completed, your company will be activated. Once your company is approved, you will be directed to the **Company Overview** page upon logging in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
