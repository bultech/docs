# Company Creation

Users who are new to the system or not yet part of a company need to create one to use the platform.

## Company Creation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Page">
    After logging in, if you don't have a company, you will be automatically redirected to the company creation page. Alternatively, you can go to `https://app.bultech.co/company/create`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Out the Form">
    - **Company Name (Required):** Enter the full name of your company.
    - **Company Email (Required):** Enter the official email address for your company.
    - **Other Information (Optional):** You can add other details like a company logo, phone number, and address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the Company">
    Complete the process by clicking the blue "Create Company" button at the bottom of the form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After creation, you will be redirected to the [Approval Process](/en/company-operations/approval-process) page.

<Note>
  **Company Owner Role:** When you create a company, you are automatically assigned the **Company Owner** role for that company. This role gives you full permissions over the company. For more details, see the [Roles and Permissions](/en/platform-rules/roles-and-permissions-rbac) section.
</Note>
