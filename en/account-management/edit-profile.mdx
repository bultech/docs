# Editing Profile Information

This section explains how to update your personal profile information (name, profile picture, title, etc.).

## Accessing the Profile Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Your User Card">
    Hover over your [user card](/en/account-management/user-card) at the bottom of the left menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Profile">
    Click the **Profile** (<img src="/icons/account-circle-outline.svg" alt="account-circle-outline" className="inline align-middle dark:invert" style={{width: '16px', height: '16px', margin: 0}} />) option from the menu that appears.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Direct URL: `https://app.bultech.co/account/profile`

## Editable Fields

You can update the following information on your profile page:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Profile Picture">
    Click on your current profile picture to upload a new one from your device.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Full Name">
    You can edit your first and last name. This name will be visible throughout the platform (in comments, assignments, etc.).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Title">
    You can enter your title or role within your company in this field.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  **Non-Editable Information:** For security reasons, the **email address** associated with your account cannot be changed from the profile page. You need to contact support for email changes.
</Note>
